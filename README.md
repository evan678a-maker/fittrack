# FitTrack - Deploy Instructions

## Netlify (easiest - drag and drop)

1. Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag the entire `fittrack` folder onto the page
3. Done! You'll get a URL like `random-name.netlify.app`
4. Open that URL on your phone
5. **iPhone**: Tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"
6. **Android**: Tap the menu (⋮) → "Add to Home screen"

That's it — it'll look and feel like a real app on your phone.

## Your data

All your progress is saved in your phone's browser storage (localStorage).
It stays there as long as you don't clear your browser data.
Data does NOT sync between devices — it's per-device.
